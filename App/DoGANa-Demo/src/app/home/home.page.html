Punta Della DoGANa: Style Transfer for Historical Images of Venice

Painting or Drawing to Photorealistic Image Conversion

In this subsection you can select a model, use our images or add your own, and then convert them automatically to a photorealistic image of Venice. Both models are our extension of a Contrastive Unpaired Translation Generative Adversarial Network which utilize edgeloss with a weighting of 0.4 and canny filter values of 350 / 450 respectively, spectral normalization on the Resnet blocks, batch normalization on the parameters, and a self-attention block before the Resnet blocks. More information is available on the model architecture on the information page.
